Who We Are and What We Do?


Maljā is a mental health platform dedicated to making therapy more accessible, secure, and user-friendly.
Our mission is to connect individuals with certified therapists and provide tools for emotional wellness.
Whether you're struggling or simply seeking growth, we're here to support your journey.



Our Users


We serve a diverse community — from teens facing emotional hurdles to adults navigating stress or change.
Therapists also join us to offer services online in a secure space.
Whether you're here for healing or to help others, Maljā welcomes you.



Our Services


- Secure access to licensed therapists.
- Personalized mental health support.
- Easy booking and scheduling.
- Educational content and emotional tools.
- Mental health awareness campaigns.



Why Maljā?


We believe in a safe space to grow and heal.
Our platform connects you with certified professionals in a user-friendly, private setting.
We aim to make therapy accessible, respectful, and tailored to your needs.



Our Policy


1. Confidentiality: We never share your data without consent.
2. Professional Standards: All therapists are certified and verified.
3. Respect: We maintain a discrimination-free, safe environment.
4. Data Security: Your data is encrypted and protected.
5. Cancellation: Reschedule or cancel according to terms.
6. Accessibility: We strive to accommodate every user's needs.
